About Us

PASSIONPERSEVERANCE PEOPLE

With 20 years immense experience in aquaculture industry, Gentils Organics is a leading manufacturer of wide range of aquaculture products. Gentils has Prawn and Fish Feed Manufacturing Unit in kondapur, Hyderabad and marketing office in Bhavanipuram, Vijayawada, in India.

Gentils produces high quality feed, consistently catering to the Indian prawn and fish farmers. We pay utmost attention to the highest quality standards. We have a diverse group of talented people who discover, develop, manufacture and market Gentils products for use around the globe. We strive hard to keep our goodwill & to gain customer's confidence.
